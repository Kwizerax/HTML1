Project 1

Green Rustic Cottage Interior Design

Nature-Inspired Palette

The foundation of this design style is a soothing palette of greens, inspired by the lush countryside. Soft sage, moss, and olive tones dominate the walls and upholstery, creating a harmonious connection with the outdoors. These shades are complemented by neutrals such as creamy whites, warm beiges, and gentle grays, enhancing the overall tranquility of the space.

Vintage Accents

To enhance the rustic charm, vintage accents play a crucial role. Antique light fixtures, weathered mirrors, and aged metal hardware bring a nostalgic touch to the interior. These elements not only add visual interest but also create a lived-in, cozy ambiance that makes the cottage feel like home.

Project 2

Poster Design and Logos

Theater Poster Design

I designed a series of promotional posters for a theater production, combining bold visuals with thematic elements to capture the essence of the show. Each poster was carefully crafted to reflect the mood, genre, and key storylines, drawing attention while maintaining a cohesive visual identity. From typography to color palettes, every detail was chosen to intrigue audiences and boost engagement both online and in print.

Mockups to show design

These posters were created to build excitement around a theater performance, blending striking imagery with expressive design to reflect the show’s energy and narrative. I aimed to visually translate the emotion and drama of the stage onto paper, using dynamic layouts, bold colors, and theatrical typography to capture attention and spark curiosity among potential audience members.

Project 3

Promotions for event in restuarant

Ladies' Night posts

The Ladies' Night posters were designed to radiate elegance, fun, and empowerment, capturing the spirit of an unforgettable evening tailored for women. Using a chic and vibrant color palette—think bold pinks, rich purples, and metallic accents—I aimed to create an eye-catching design that instantly conveyed both celebration and sophistication. Stylish typography and modern graphic elements gave the posters a trendy, upscale feel while staying approachable and energetic.

Eye-catching elements and text

Each version of the poster was crafted with attention to detail, balancing glamour with clarity. Key event information was presented prominently, while decorative flourishes, such as silhouettes, sparkles, or flowing lines, added flair without overwhelming the message. Whether displayed online or around town, the Ladies' Night posters were designed to stand out, attract the right crowd, and build anticipation for a night of connection, laughter, and great memories.
